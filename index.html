About The Station

Strategic Location

Business Bay Metro Station is a key station on the Red Line of the Dubai Metro. Located in the heart of Dubai's business district, this station serves as an important transport hub for professionals, residents, and tourists alike.

Situated on Sheikh Zayed Road, the station provides convenient access to numerous corporate offices, residential towers, and entertainment venues in the Business Bay area.

Station Facilities

Ticket Counters

Staffed ticket offices for Nol card purchase and recharge. Automated ticket machines also available for quick self-service.

Free Wi-Fi

Complimentary high-speed wireless internet throughout the station to keep you connected during your journey.

Retail Shops

Convenience stores and retail outlets offering snacks, refreshments, and travel essentials.

Cafés

Various coffee shops and eateries offering refreshments and quick meals for travelers on the go.

Accessibility

Elevators, escalators, and facilities for people of determination to ensure comfortable travel for everyone.

Park & Ride

Ample parking facilities allowing commuters to park their vehicles and continue their journey via metro.

Nearby Attractions

Dubai Water Canal

10 minutes walking distance

A 3.2km long waterway extending from the Creek in Old Dubai through to Business Bay, ending in the Arabian Gulf. Features waterfall bridges, cycling paths, and recreational areas.

Bay Avenue Mall

5 minutes walking distance

A community retail destination in Business Bay featuring restaurants, cafés, supermarkets, and various retail outlets for shopping and entertainment.

Dubai Design District (d3)

15 minutes by taxi

A creative hub dedicated to design, fashion, and art, featuring galleries, design studios, and trendy restaurants in a modern setting.

Burj Khalifa & Dubai Mall

1 metro station away

The world's tallest building and one of the largest shopping malls are just one metro stop away at Dubai Mall/Burj Khalifa Station.

Transport Options

Feeder Buses

  • Bus F13: Business Bay - Al Khail Gate
  • Bus F14: Business Bay - Executive Towers
  • Bus F16: Business Bay - Marasi Drive
  • Frequency: Every 10-15 minutes during peak hours

Taxis & Ride Sharing

  • Dedicated taxi rank at station exits
  • Uber and Careem pick-up points
  • Women-only pink taxis available
  • 24-hour taxi service with short waiting times

Pedestrian Access

  • Pedestrian bridges connecting to both sides of Sheikh Zayed Road
  • Air-conditioned walkways during summer months
  • Connected to nearby buildings via covered walkways
  • Wheelchair accessible pathways throughout
